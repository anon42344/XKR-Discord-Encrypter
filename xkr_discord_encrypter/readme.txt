XKR-Discord-Encrypter
Go to chrome://extensions, turn on "Developer mode" select "Load unpacked" and select the directory with files. Navigate in your browser to a discord channel. Press the icon to activate. After you typed your message, press § (usually under esc button) to encrypt a message. For more security: Enter a password for your server, and add an optional password for your channel.

Part of the kryptokrona project .
